About Us

Siam Jem is a female-fronted band, from Los Angeles, CA, that combines the atmospheric qualities of shoegaze with an edgy, punk-inspired attitude, creating a sound they refer to as “dream punk.”

The band has spent the last year finishing up their newest release, featuring songs such as newest single “Colossus”. They also embarked on a 7 show tour of Japan in September of 2024 including stops in Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka, and Fukuoka.

Siam Jem has previously shared the stage with multiple up-and-coming artists including Sir Chloe, Levitation Room, and Winona Forever.

Siam Jem is Mika Rabina on vocals/rhythm guitar, Minli Flynn on bass, Tommy Sweet on drums/back up vocals , & Dane Mora on lead guitar/back up vocals.
